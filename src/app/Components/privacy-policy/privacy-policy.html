Privacy Policy - Click Site



1. General
Click Site is committed to protecting user privacy in accordance with the Israel Protection of Privacy Law, 1981.

2. Data Collection We collect information provided voluntarily by the user during registration or purchase, including name, email address, and contact details.

3. Use of Information Collected data is used solely for processing transactions, providing technical support, and sending service updates or marketing materials (subject to user consent).

4. Data Security We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard user information. However, absolute security against unauthorized access cannot be guaranteed.

5. Payment Security Credit card information is NOT stored on our servers. All payments are processed through authorized third-party payment gateways compliant with PCI-DSS standards.

6. Disclosure to Third Parties We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to facilitate technical service delivery (e.g., email service providers).

7. Jurisdiction This policyis governed by the laws of the State of Israel, with exclusive jurisdiction granted to the courts of the Jerusalem District.

8.Activity Logging The system maintains an internal audit log that automatically records user activities (such as login times, purchases, and prompt usage). This information is collected for purposes of data security, technical troubleshooting, fraud prevention, and service optimization.
